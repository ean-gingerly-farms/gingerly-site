Our Story

Why Gingerly Farms

Gingerly Farms started because we wanted to grow fresh greens in Charleston, year-round. Not shipped from across the country. Not dependent on weather or season. Grown right here, delivered the same day.

We're building this from the ground up, focused on hydroponic production that's scalable and rooted in this community. Year one is about growing the farm and earning the trust of the restaurants, wholesalers, and markets we serve. The long game is scaling production, acquiring property, and expanding what a local farm can look like.

Hydroponics is the foundation. Local restaurants are the customers. Scale is the plan. We're keeping it simple and building from there.
