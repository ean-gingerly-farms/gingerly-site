Our Story

Why Gingerly Farms

Gingerly Farms started with a straightforward belief: Charleston deserves access to fresh, locally grown greens year-round. Not shipped from across the country. Not dependent on weather or season. Grown right here, delivered the same day.

We're building this from the ground up — focused on hydroponic production that's sustainable, scalable, and rooted in this community. Year one is about growing the farm and earning the trust of the restaurants, wholesalers, and markets we serve. The long game is scaling production, acquiring property, and expanding what a local farm can look like.

We're not just growing greens — we're building the infrastructure for a sustainable local food system. Hydroponics is the foundation. Community is the purpose. Scale is the plan.
