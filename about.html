Our Story

Why Gingerly Farms

Gingerly Farms started with a simple idea: what if we grew the ingredients for our own beverages? Not as a marketing story, but as a real operational advantage. We grow the ginger. We brew the drinks. We control the whole chain.

We're two 26-year-olds building this alongside full-time jobs. It's scrappy and capital-light by design. We're not chasing venture money or trying to be a lifestyle brand. We're building a system that works — where the farm feeds the product line and the product line funds the farm.

We are not just a beverage brand or a farm. We are a vertically integrated system where we grow inputs and produce outputs. The farm is the long-term advantage.
