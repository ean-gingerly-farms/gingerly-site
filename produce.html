The Vision

Scaling Toward Vertical

We started with hydroponic growing because it's efficient, sustainable, and produces consistent results year-round. The long-term vision is vertical indoor farming with stacked systems that multiply our output in the same footprint.

Hydroponics uses less water, eliminates soil-borne disease, and lets us grow in controlled conditions regardless of weather. That means better greens for our partners and a farm built to scale.
