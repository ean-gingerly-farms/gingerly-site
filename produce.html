The Vision

Scaling Toward Vertical

We started with horizontal tray farming because it's practical, capital-light, and gets us growing immediately. But the long-term vision is vertical farming — stacked systems that multiply our output in the same footprint.

The farm isn't just a supply chain — it's the strategic advantage. Growing our own inputs means we control quality, reduce costs over time, and build something that scales with us.
